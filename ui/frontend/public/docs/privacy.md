# Privacy

Evidence Lab does not require users to create accounts to browse documents.

We use privacy-respecting analytics (Google Analytics) to understand how the platform is used and to improve performance and usability. Analytics data is collected only with user consent.

The information collected may include anonymized IP addresses, page views, and basic device information. We do not use analytics data for advertising or cross-site tracking.

Analytics data may be processed by Google LLC, including on servers outside the European Union, under appropriate legal safeguards.

You can withdraw consent at any time via the cookie settings on this site.

If you have questions about privacy or data handling, contact us at evidence-lab@astrobagel.com
